Everything captured, nothing missed

Comprehensive browser event capture for AI-assisted debugging

Console Logs

console.log(), .warn(), .error() with full argument serialization including objects and arrays.

Network Errors

Failed API calls (4xx, 5xx) with request method, URL, status code, and response body. Headers automatically sanitized.

Exceptions

Uncaught errors and unhandled promise rejections with full stack traces, source file, and line numbers.

100% Local

Logs never leave your machine. Server binds to localhost only. No cloud, no analytics, no telemetry. Fully open source.

Built for the AI-native workflow

Modern development with AI assistants is transformative, but there's friction. When your app throws an error, you screenshot it, copy the stack trace, paste it into your AI chat, and hope you captured enough context.

Gasoline eliminates that friction. Your AI assistant gets the same visibility into browser events that you do—automatically, in real-time, with full context.

It's the debugging experience AI-assisted development deserves.
